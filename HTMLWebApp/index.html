Oculus On Android

The Oculus Rift will be usable on Android devices. Smart phones and tablet which have Andoid 4.3 or higher will be able to use this function. The creators of the Oculus Rift would like to be the first Virtual Reality developers who will have a VR headset for mobile phones. The ability to use the Oculus Rift with a portable device opens up a lot of opportunities for the film and entertainment industry.
